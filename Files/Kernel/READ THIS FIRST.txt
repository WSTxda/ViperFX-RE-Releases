IRSes in this pack *must be used with V4A 2.5.0.4 or above*

Disable all other surround effects, including Dolby Atmos, V4A Field Surround, Differential Surround, Headphone Surround+ and Reverberation

Open ViPER4Android while music is playing through your headphones and check the Driver Status and note down the Sample Rate (this will 90% of the time be 48000, otherwise 44100) and choose the IRSes with the corresponding number.
